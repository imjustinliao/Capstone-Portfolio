The User

College students who want real connections — not the exhausting performance of a perfectly curated profile.

User persona image placeholder

User Profile

Primary user: undergraduate college students, ages 18–24, who are open to romantic connections and want an app experience that feels lightweight, honest, and safe.

Needs & Goals

Users need confidence that who they match with is who they say they are. They want low-friction onboarding, no resume-style profiles, and conversations that can realistically lead to meeting in real life.

Design Implications

The onboarding flow must surface authenticity immediately. A guided selfie capture replaces the typical photo upload, setting a tone of realness from the very first interaction.
