Capstone Experience

From identifying the problem to delivering a functional prototype — a reflection on the process, pivots, and decisions that shaped Serene.

Journey timeline image placeholder

Process

The project followed a human-centered design process: competitive analysis, user research, low-fidelity wireframes, iterative prototyping, and usability testing across multiple rounds.

Challenges

Balancing simplicity with enough structure to convey trust was the central design tension. Reducing onboarding friction without sacrificing the authenticity requirement required several rethinks of the Polaroid-capture flow.

Milestones

Key milestones included finalizing the user persona, completing the first interactive prototype, and conducting usability sessions that validated the core selfie-onboarding concept.
