Overall Capstone

A high-level synthesis of the Serene project — what was built, what was learned, and where it could go next.

Project summary image placeholder

Summary

Serene is a college-focused dating app that replaces the typical photo-upload flow with a guided selfie capture during onboarding, making authenticity the foundation of every match.

Key Takeaways

The most significant insight: users respond positively to products that reduce pressure rather than add it. Simplicity and trust are not trade-offs — they reinforce each other.

Next Steps

Future directions include expanding the matching algorithm, integrating campus-specific features, and conducting a broader usability study with a more diverse student population.
